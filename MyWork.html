Electrical Projects:

In my free time I have designed some embedded electronics projects with microcontrollers such as MSP430, ATmega16A, Arduino board, and Raspberry Pi. Through these projects I implemented different concepts of ADC, Comparator, UART/SPI/I2C, Timer. Some of my projects are listed below.

A musical Snake Game including:

  • MSP430 microcontrollers
  • 8 by 8 led matrix
  • Joy Stick
  • Speaker to play music

Line follower Robot including

  • Arduino UNO (or Arduino Nano)
  • LCD
  • DC motors
  • L293D Motor Driver IC
  • Ultrasonic sensor to avoid the obstacle
  • IR sensors
